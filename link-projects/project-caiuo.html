Caiuo

About

CAIUO (formerly known as YourGuide Tutorials & Services,) is an ed-tech startup in the Philippines that promotes lifelong learning and sustainable practices, mainly in the field of technology. They provide personalized educational tutorials & tailor-fit learning services. The purpose of this case study competition is to create a business proposal for CAIUO and redesign their website to help stand out as a Filipino owned business. This case study is ranked 3rd place and received Kreativity Award. It was held on March 11-12, 2022.

Role

I joined as solo and was grouped with 3 random participants named Justin Clarisse Ballesta, Jersy Anne Bapilar, and Cloyd Niere. I worked as a Lead UI/UX Designer, Digital Graphic Illustrator, and Researcher

Takeaways

Summary

During the 2-day event, I was able to be grouped with three other members and was able to maximize the use of Figma from Brainstorming to creating Information Architecture and User Interfaces of the Website.

We are very happy and pleased to receive an award from KadaKareer and CAIUO as the 3rd place of HacKada: Business Challenge Competition and awarded also as the team with the most creative design - Kreativity Award.

Our team (KadaGroupie) was able to create solutions that can help CAIUO standout from other similar ed-tech platforms or applications.

Challenges

Short Period

We are only given 24 hours to finish our final output, which includes creating user interfaces, graphic illustrations and presentation.

User Navigation

We have to consider how the users can navigate through the app easily given that the original website has many items from the navigation bar

Lessons Learned

  • I was able to team up with random people and find our strengths to quickly finish the case study.
  • As we have experienced navigating through the original website, we were able to determine on how we could organize the contents within the website.
