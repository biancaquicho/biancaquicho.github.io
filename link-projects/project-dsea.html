DSEA

About

The official website for DSEA, a Swedish company specializing in stone crushing, installation work, and troubleshooting, was designed and developed as part of a work project. It was launched on June 2024. The website features a modern, clean design aimed at showcasing DSEA's services while offering easy navigation for users. The project included implementing animated elements, streamlined fonts, and a functional contact page to enhance user engagement.

Problem Statement

DSEA required a website that reflected their professional image and effectively communicated their services. The previous site lacked clarity, modern design elements, and user-friendly features, making it difficult for visitors to access key information like the company’s services, team, and contact details. The client also wanted to incorporate animation and dynamic elements to make the website more interactive.

Objective

To create a modern, user-friendly website for DSEA that effectively highlights their core services, enhances their professional image, and engages users through clean design, intuitive navigation, and interactive elements.

Solution

The redesigned website for DSEA incorporated a clean and modern aesthetic with a sans-serif font, moving away from the cursive style previously used. The homepage was enhanced to showcase their three core services—stone crushing, installation work, and troubleshooting—using dynamic animations for better engagement. The contact page featured a professional layout with staff profiles, photos, and contact details, while the footer was optimized for clear navigation. These updates ensured a professional, engaging, and user-focused experience.
