Olssons Bil

About

The official website for Olssons Bil, a car dealership in Norrköping, Sweden, was designed and developed to create a seamless and modern platform. It was launched on March 2024. The site highlights their vehicle services while ensuring an engaging and user-friendly experience for visitors.

Problem Statement

The original website lacked modern features, had an outdated design, and provided a subpar user experience. It struggled to highlight Olssons Bil's broad inventory and services effectively, limiting its ability to attract and retain online customers.

Objective

To redesign and develop a website that enhances user experience, modernizes the visual identity, and effectively communicates the company's services. The new platform needed to be user-friendly, mobile-responsive, and optimized for showcasing the available car inventory.

Solution

A fresh, visually appealing website was developed, incorporating intuitive navigation and mobile responsiveness. Features like dynamic car listings, streamlined service descriptions, and integrated contact forms were implemented. The updated design also emphasized branding elements to reinforce trust and professionalism while providing a seamless browsing experience across all devices.
